INSTALLATION:

Run Winzip and unzip kitkat_urban.zip.  Open the kitkat_urban folder and place the kitkat_urban.bsp, kitkat_urban.res, and kitkat_urban.txt in your valve/maps folder.  Place the chaoscity.tga files into your valve/gfx/env folder.  Place the wav files in a folder called urban and place that in your valve/sounds folder.   Then place the moneygibs.mdl in your valve/models folder.  Also place the kitkat_urban overviews in your valve/overviews folder.

If you'd like a copy of the kitkat_urban.wad, email me and I'll send it to you.  docrock64@cox.net
